Baptism
At least once of the parents must be a practising Roman Catholic here at St. Joseph's, registered for at least 9 months, or living within the area of this parish. Baptisms are done every third Sunday of the month. Forms are available in the office.
First Communion Classes
Classes take place in the Church Hall during the 11:00 am Mass, from October to April.
Confirmation Classes
Classes take place on Sunday from 10:00 am to 10:45 am, October to April.
Marriage
Registered parishoners only. Please contact the office one year in advance, a marriage preparation course is required.
R.C.I.A
Classes take place Thursday evenings at 7:30 pm, from October to April. Call the parish as soon as possible if you want to become a Roman Catholic.
Hospital Visit or Sacrament Of The Sick
Please contact the parish office to notify the priests if a family member is sick at home or in hospital and would like a visit.
