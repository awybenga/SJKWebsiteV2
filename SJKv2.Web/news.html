Novena for Safe Return to School

Bishop Crosby is leading our Diocese in a Novena for a safe return to school. We begin on August 31, and finish September 8, the first day of school for students. The full Novena is available as a PDF from the Diocese. Each day we will post the daily prayers on social media. On these 9 days of prayer we will join together: parishes and schools, staff and students, parents and grandparents, parishioners and community members. We will pray for the protection of all, for the peace of all, and for wisdom and courage for us all.
