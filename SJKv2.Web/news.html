Vacation Bible School

Registration is now open for Vacation Bible School. This year's theme is Weird Animals - where Jesus' love is one-of-a-kind!
This year VBS will run July 15-19, 8:50 am - 12:30 pm at St Joseph Church.
Each child needs a completed registration form and media waiver. Registrations can be dropped off at the parish office, put in the mailbox, or put in an envelope in the collection basket marked VBS.
VBS is open to those starting grade 1 through grade 6 in September. Anyone who will finish grade 6 or above this year is welcome to volunteer to help lead our small group "zoo crews" or stations. This camp needs a wide variety of skills: sports and athletics, music, science, drama, dance, leadership - there's a place for everyone! If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to call the parish or email Mary at stjosephkitcheneryouth@gmail.com
