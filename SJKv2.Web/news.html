Novena Prayer to the Sacred Heart, June 3 to 11

The priests serving our parishes belong to a religious community, the Comboni Missionaries: the initials are MCCJ, Latin for Missionari Comboniani Cordis Jesu, that is, Comboni Missionaries of the Heart of Jesus. They were founded by St. Daniel Comboni and are guided by the founder’s charism and faithfulness to the Sacred Heart of Jesus. As parish faith communities, we join our priests in the Novena Prayer to the Sacred Heart. We are deeply convinced that the grace of the Sacred Heart will help us conquer all obstacles, especially the pandemic affecting our globe. “Courage for the present and especially in the future.” (St. Daniel Comboni)
