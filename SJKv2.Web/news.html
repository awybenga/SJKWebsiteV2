Mass Times Survey
Hamilton Diocese has recommended that St. Joseph's should, along with several other parishes, reduce the number of Sunday Masses being celebrated. We would like to get your feedback on this issue. A survey will be distributed at all masses on the weekend of May 19th and 20th.

This survey is intended for those who regularly pray at St Joseph’s, and are either confirmed or over 18 years old. Please submit your responses by 11:59 pm on Sunday, June 3rd.

If you did not get a copy, you can download a PDF version of the survey here. The pdf version can be returned to the Parish Office or put in the collection basket.

You can also complete the survey online here.
