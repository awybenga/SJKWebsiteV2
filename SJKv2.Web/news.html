Relic of Saint Andre Bessett

March 8 is a special day for our parish. We will host the relic of Saint Andre Bessett (Bro. Andre of St. Joseph's Oratory fame in Montreal) He had a very special devotion to St. Joseph. We will have an explanation and Veneration of the relic, followed by Mass and then the blessing with the relic. It all starts at 6:00 pm next Tuesday.
